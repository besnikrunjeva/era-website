## 7. Order & Sales Process

### Current system (as-is):
1. Client contacts Uncle Agja via Viber or WhatsApp
2. Agja posts order confirmation to Viber group "Porositë e Konfirmuara"
3. Production team sees message and begins work
4. Order delivered — no formal tracking
5. Invoice issued via Excel file (faturimi_ERA_2025.xlsm)

### Weaknesses:
- No CRM — all client data in Agja's phone
- No follow-up system for dormant clients
- No minimum order policy
- No proactive outbound sales
- Marketing is inactive (Instagram/social media)
- Revenue tracking only via invoicing, not order confirmations

### Order format in Viber (example):
```
Gota 7oz Auto styling 70.000 cop 1800€, Naim Hasani whatsap, kujdes logon era posht mja jek
```
Fields typically: Product + Client brand name + Quantity + Price (sometimes) + Contact name + Channel (viber/whatsap)

## 12. Business Growth Story

ERA has grown from a small local printer to a serious regional packaging manufacturer over 9 years:

- **2017–2019:** Early stage, €73K–€167K. Mostly local Ferizaj clients, traditional printing.
- **2020:** Breakout year — revenue more than doubled to €305K. Likely when food packaging/cups became the focus.
- **2021–2022:** Continued growth to €451K. Cup machines and pasta cup line established.
- **2023:** First decline (-6% to €423K) — only down year on record. Likely market saturation or competition.
- **2024:** Strong rebound +48% to €624K. Pasta Fasta and food delivery packaging boom.
- **2025:** On track for €768K (+23%). Business is at its strongest point ever.

**The trajectory is clear:** ERA is not a struggling small business — it is a growing mid-sized manufacturer with real infrastructure, real clients, and nearly 10× revenue growth in under a decade. The opportunity for the next CEO (Besnik) is to professionalize the commercial operation to match the production capability that already exists.
